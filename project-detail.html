Boutique Hotel / Çukurcuma

Project Status: Design Development Phase

Site Analysis

Concept

As one of Istanbul’s oldest neighborhoods, Çukurcuma continues to preserve its historical essence through its heritage buildings and antique shops. The design of this boutique hotel aims to immerse guests in this storied atmosphere; it offers a seamless transition from wandering the historic streets to staying within them. The primary objective is to allow visitors to wake up to the timeless scenery of Çukurcuma, experiencing history with modern comfort.
